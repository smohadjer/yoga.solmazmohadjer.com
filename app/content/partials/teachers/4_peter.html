Peter Kloos

An avid athlete and outdoorsman, Peter's first experience with yoga came in 2010. He was drawn to the increased strength, flexibility, concentration and awareness that a dedicated yoga practice offers. For him, yoga is a way of staying grounded and balanced. His teaching style is down to earth and slow-paced, with an emphasis on relaxation and mindfulness.

  • InFö, Tübingen, Germany, 05/2016 - 02/2020
