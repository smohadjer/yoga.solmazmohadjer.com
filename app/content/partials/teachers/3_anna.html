Anna Mohadjer

Anna aims to bring a sense of discipline, focus and playfulness into her classes. She is certified in Flow, Power, and Hatha yoga. In her free time, Anna likes to study languages, spend time outdoors, and figure skate. She enjoys the physical challenge of yoga and uses it to complement her figure skating training.
